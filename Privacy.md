# Privacy Policy for "Sensei on my Side" Chrome Extension

**Last Updated: May 20, 2026**

This privacy policy governs your use of the Chrome extension "Sensei on my Side" (the "Extension").

The Extension is designed as an English learning assistant. We deeply value your privacy, and this policy outlines how we handle data.

---

### 1. Data Collection and Processing (Local AI Execution)

The Extension utilizes Google Chrome's built-in **Gemini Nano** (built-in AI) to process text and provide English learning assistance. 

* **On-Device Processing:** All text generation, processing, and analysis are performed **strictly on your local device**. 
* **No Server Storage:** Your input data (sentences you read, write, or translate) and the AI-generated responses are never sent to, processed by, or stored on any external servers owned by us or third parties.

### 2. Chrome Storage API

The Extension may use Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local` or `chrome.storage.sync`) to save your application settings, preferences, or learning history. This data remains within your Chrome browser environment and is not transmitted externally.

### 3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

We do not use any third-party tracking tools, advertising networks, or analytics services. Your activities within the Extension remain completely private to you.

### 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
